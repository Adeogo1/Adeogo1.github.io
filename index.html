Portfolio

LEAP.LEARN.LIVE!

LEAP.LEARN.LIVE is a 2D endless platformer where you control a bouncing ball to move left and right, dodge obstacles, and avoid falling to your death. You can also collect coins which can be used to buy items in the store. This was created on unity using C#.

Around The World

Around The World is a 2D endless runner as well, where you play as a player who can double jump and there are also different maps with different Levels The player has to jump over obstacles and survive, you can also collect coins which can be used to buy items in the store. This was created on unity using C# .

Swerve

Swerve is a 2D endless game as well, where you play as a ball who has to dodge obstacles and survive, you can also collect coins which can be used to buy items in the store. This was created on unity using C# .

Goals

We aim to build 2D games to 2.5D games to 3D games on mobile and then finally transition to console games for ages 12 and upwards. We feel as though games are just being made by big companies and for players and gamers to just accept what they are given and make do with what they have with little or no improvement geared towards the players wants but we aim to change that and create YOUR game. A game for you because what's most important to us is your legacy.
